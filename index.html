Bugaboo Donkey

The Bugaboo Donkey is the first convertible pushchair that can convert from a mono to a duo pushchair and back again in three simple clicks, allowing you to adapt it to suit your family life, and making getting around with your children really easy. Features include height adjustable leatherette handlebar, 5-point harness, multi-terrain air filled rear wheels, front swivel wheels, reclinable and reversible seats, underseat basket and side luggage basket (capacity up to 22lbs).
